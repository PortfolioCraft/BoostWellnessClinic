Discover Sustainable Weight Loss with Our Medically Supervised Weight loss Program

Tired of the Weight Loss Rollercoaster?

You're not the only one on this journey. Our medically supervised weight loss program is designed specifically for individuals seeking a lasting and effective solution to improve their health by shedding excess weight.
We offer a comprehensive and structured approach to creating a wellness plan dedicated to achieving your optimal body goals.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) -


Q: What is a Medically supervised Weight Loss Program?

A: A Medically Supervised Weight Loss Program is a comprehensive weight management plan designed to help you achieve and maintain a healthy weight through balanced nutrition, physical activity, and lifestyle changes. It's focused on sustainable health and well-being, rather than quick fixes.


Q: Who is the program for?

A: This program is ideal for anyone looking to lose weight in a healthy, sustainable way, whether you're just starting your weight loss journey or seeking to maintain your current healthy weight.


Q: What does the program include?

A: The program includes personalized nutrition plans, fitness routines, regular health assessments, and ongoing support from our team of medical providers, fitness experts, and health coaches.


Q: Does the program include the use of FDA-approved medication for weight loss?

A: Yes, for eligible participants, our program may incorporate FDA-approved weight loss medications as part of the treatment plan. These medications are used in conjunction with nutritional guidance, fitness routines, and lifestyle modifications, and are prescribed after a thorough health evaluation to ensure they are appropriate and safe for you.


Q: How long is the program?

A: The duration of the program varies depending on your individual goals and needs. We offer flexible plans ranging from a few months to a year-long journey.


Q: Is the program based on dieting?

A: Rather than promoting short-term diets, the program focuses on long-term lifestyle changes that include balanced eating, regular physical activity, and mindfulness practices.


Q: Do I need to purchase special foods or supplements?

A: No, the program emphasizes eating whole, nutritious foods that are easily accessible. We may recommend certain supplements if they fit your individual health needs, but they are not a mandatory part of the program.


Q: What hormones are most linked to weight gain?

A: Hormones such as cortisol (stress hormone), insulin (related to sugar metabolism), thyroid hormones, and sex hormones like estrogen and testosterone can all impact weight. Imbalances in these hormones can lead to weight gain or difficulty in losing weight.


Q: How does Boost Wellness Clinic approach weight loss relate to hormone imbalance?

A: At Boost Wellness Clinic, we offer personalized assessments to identify hormone imbalances that may be affecting weight. Our comprehensive approach includes hormone therapy (if needed), nutritional counseling, fitness programs, and lifestyle modifications for effective weight management.


Q: Can the program accommodate dietary restrictions?

A: Yes, we can tailor your nutrition plan to accommodate any dietary restrictions, allergies, or preferences you have.


Q: What kind of support will I receive?

A: You will receive continuous support from our team, including regular check-ins, progress tracking, and adjustments to your plan as needed. We're here to motivate and guide you throughout your journey.


Q: How is progress measured in the program?

A: Progress is measured through a combination of weight tracking, body measurements, fitness level assessments, and overall health and well-being indicators.


Q: Is the weight Loss Program covered by insurance?

A: Coverage for weight loss programs varies by insurance provider and plan. We recommend checking with your insurance provider to understand your coverage. Our staff can assist you in navigating insurance queries and provide information on available payment options for any uncovered services.


Q: How can I sign up for the weight Loss Program?

A: You can sign up by contacting us directly through our website or by calling our clinic. We'll start with an initial consultation to discuss your goals and how we can best support you.



Have More Questions? Feel free to reach out to us for more information or to schedule your initial consultation. Let's take the first step on your journey together!
