Who We Are

At Boost Wellness Clinic, we blend the principles of lifestyle medicine with a targeted approach to medical weight loss. Here, we don't just focus on shedding pounds; we aim to enhance your overall well-being.


Our Approach

At Boost Wellness Clinic, we understand that weight management is a complex journey influenced by a variety of factors. We believe that sustainable weight loss starts with understanding the underlying reasons for weight gain. Our approach involves exploring factors like metabolic health, hormonal imbalances, lifestyle habits, and emotional well-being to create a comprehensive and effective plan for you. By combining lifestyle medicine with FDA-approved medications, we create an approach that's as unique as you are.

Our Amazing Services


Meal Plans

We provide customized nutritional plans addressing dietary needs and preferences and ensuring a balanced intake for optimal health and weight management.


Exercise Plans

Our clinic offers diverse fitness programs, including personal training, group classes, and specialized exercise plans, all designed to enhance weight loss and promote physical well-being.


Behavioral and Lifestyle Coaching

Understanding the psychological aspects of eating and weight management is crucial. Our coach services focus on behavior modification, stress management, and developing healthy lifestyle habits.


Advanced Medical Weight Loss Solutions:

We utilize FDA-approved medications and other medical interventions as part of our comprehensive weight management strategy, ensuring safe and effective treatment under professional supervision.


Hormonal Balance and Metabolic Health Assessments

Our team evaluates hormonal and metabolic factors that may influence weight, providing treatments and recommendations to address these underlying issues.


Nutritional Deficiency Analysis and Correction

We assess and correct nutritional deficiencies, understanding their role in weight management and overall health.
